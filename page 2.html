A Passion Turned
Legacy

Waggenburg Kennels began with a single Golden Retriever who changed our lives. We are dedicated to preserving the breed's finest qualities—its gentle nature, intelligence, and unwavering loyalty—for other families to experience.

Excellence In Every Generation

We adhere to the strictest health and genetic testing protocols. Our focus is on producing puppies with sound temperaments and robust health, ensuring they are a source of joy for years to come

Total transparency is our standard. We provide all health clearances, OFA results, and pedigrees to every prospective family as part of our commitment to trust.

and Expertise
